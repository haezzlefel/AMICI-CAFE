About Us

AMICI CAFE offers a unique coffee house environment. We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee, we are a place where you can sit down and enjoy that tailor-made cup of coffee. If you need to work, we have a seating area created specifically for you. If you need to rest, we have a soft-seating area in front of a stone couch that is perfect for your weary mind and body. We offer a delicious variety of coffee from Baguio, roasted beans made by our professionally trained baristas. We have everything from classic coffee to our house made specialty beverages. All of our sauces & syrups are made in-house with all natural ingredients (no chemicals or preservatives) ensuring you the highest quality in taste & health. You can complete your coffee with one of our delicious sweet treats made by our very own baker. We look forward to serving you at Amici Cafe.
